Addressing Authority

We assign and maintain road names and physical addresses for all structures in Carbon County — primarily to support 9-1-1 and emergency services.

To apply for a new address, use the County Development Permit Package Application.

Data Disclaimer

Geographic data published here represents the best available information at the time of publication. Carbon County makes no warranties regarding accuracy or completeness for any particular purpose. Users assume all responsibility for decisions made using this information.

Printed Maps

Printed versions of county-produced maps are available at a cost-recovery price. Map costs are determined by size, paper type, and finishing options such as lamination. Contact the department for current pricing.
