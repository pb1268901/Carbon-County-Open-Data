Address Lookup

Search the Carbon County 9-1-1 address database to verify whether a physical address is on record. Enter a street address below to check. This database is maintained by the GIS & Addressing Department for emergency services purposes.

Enter your street address. Add a community name to narrow results — e.g. "Red Lodge", "Bridger", "Joliet".

About This Database

This tool queries the official Carbon County NG911 site address point (SSAP) database. Not all rural or newly constructed addresses may appear. If your address is not found, use the report button to notify the GIS & Addressing Department. To apply for a new address, use the County Development Permit Package Application.

About & Contact

GIS & Addressing Department — Carbon County, Montana

Addressing Authority

The GIS & Addressing Department serves as the addressing authority for Carbon County. We maintain the official database of road names and physical addresses for all structures within the County, primarily in support of 9-1-1 and emergency services.

To apply for a new physical address, use the online County Development Permit Package Application.
